# Aram Lee

PhD Student in Astronomy · University of British Columbia

I am a PhD student at UBC working on detection pipelines for Trans-Neptunian Objects (TNOs), supervised by JJ Kavelaars. My research focuses on pushing the limits of automated detection methods for small, distant solar system bodies in the outer Kuiper Belt.

Previously, I completed my MSc at the University of Victoria (The best city).

## Research Interests

- TNO discovery methods
- Kuiper Belt Object demographics
- Solar system formation and evolution
- TNO composition, surface, and interior
